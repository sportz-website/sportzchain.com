OUR Roadmap

Q1 2022

  • Minimum 4 exclusive sporting team partnerships
  • Launch sign-up drive and whitelist program
  • Smart contract Certik audit and deployment
  • First NFT launch of partnership teams

Q2 2022

  • Launch IEO / IDO and Listing of $SPN on key mid-tier exchanges
  • Launch of WebdApp with swap functionality for trading sports tokens
  • Launching Sports Token Offering (STO) of selected partnership teams
  • Launching staking mechanism for $SPN, partner and non-partner team tokens

Q3 2022

  • Multiple STO launches and launching Gamefi on the platform
  • Launching full fledged proprietary exchange for trading of sports tokens
  • in-house NFT auction marketplace
  • Expanding sporting team partnerships (Cricket, Football, eSports)

Q4 2022

  • Expanding active user base across Asia
  • Launch of Talent Token product
  • Completion and launch of Mobile App with AR/VR Game
  • Listing $SPN on big tier exchanges such as Binance, Kucoin and FTX

Q1 2023

  • Achieving 15+ exclusive team partnerships across sporting verticals
  • Integration of $SPN and Team Tokens on metaverse like Sandbox
  • Listing partnered team sports tokens on prominent third party exchanges
  • Listing $SPN on big tier exchanges such as Binance, Kucoin and FTX

Q2 2023

  • Launching full-fledged fan engagement features such as Fan Clubs, P2P games, etc.
  • Aggregating and providing access to exclusive sporting contents

  • Initiating development of Side Chain and DeFI programs
