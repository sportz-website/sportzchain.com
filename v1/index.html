What is SportZchain?

SportZchain is a blockchain-powered platform for fans to crowd-manage their favourite teams, games, leagues, events, and more. A platform that provides fans a direct power to not only follow & interact but actively ENGAGE and PARTICIPATE in various binding and non-binding decisions of the team

SportZchain provides sports teams & leagues with blockchain-based tools to help them better engage & monetize their audiences. We help sports team to create ‘INVESTED FAN’ base and declutter noise present in existing social media platforms

$SPN is the booster for
SportZchain platform

SportZchain platform and fan engagement app will be built on blockchain infrastructure. $SPN will be an exclusive native on-platform currency for SportZchain ecosystem.

SportZchain platform will partner with various sporting clubs (Football, Cricket, NBA, Baseball, WWE, etc.) to launch their branded Sports Token Offerings and $SPN will be used to purchase the branded Sports tokens on SportZchain proprietary exchange

Our Mission

To provide MULTIPLE UTILITIES for $SPN holders and make it a native asset that is interoperable across sports Ecosystem

Sports Token Offering

Sports token offerings are launched by partnered sporting teams on SportZchain platform. Through branded sports tokens, fan acquire VIP PRIVILEGES AND VOTING RIGHTS to actively participate in various team decisions.

Gamification

SportZchain will launch various active and passive games (including localized augment reality games) on the platform which will further increase fan engagement as well as provide opportunity for fans to EARN REWARDS in $SPN or various branded sports tokens

NFT MarketPlace

Obtaining original signed sporting gears is a challenge. SportZchain plans to launch an NFT auction marketplace where sporting celebrities will auction their signed gears directly to the fans. These gears will be authenticated on the blockchain providing the PROOF OF TRUST

App Monetization

SportZchain will further enhance the ecosystem by launching in-app stores, offering sponsored content and strive to make $SPN as a sporting ecosystem universal token providing fans MULTIPLE AVENUES to utilize the crypto asset
